CELINE Core Ontology: Description back to ToC

The CELINE Core Ontology is a lightweight orchestration profile for Renewable Energy Communities (RECs) and Digital Twin workflows. It connects established standards — PECO, SAREF, SAREF4ENER, SOSA/SSN and BIGG — without redefining their domain semantics, providing a minimal coordination layer for simulation scenarios, dataset references, KPI evaluation, and REC flexibility commitment and settlement.
